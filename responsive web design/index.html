About Me

I am Dr. Mesbah Kamal and I'm a

Chairperson Research And Development Collective (RDC) Coordinator, Technical Committee Parliamentary Caucus On Indigenous Peoples Secretary General National Coalition For Indigenous Peoples (NCIP) Convener Bangladesh Manabadhikar Andolan (Bangladesh Campaign For Human Rights) Joint Convener Bangladesh Itihas Sammilani (Bangladesh History Congress) Chief Adviser Bangladesh Dalit Forum Member, Expert Committee Bharat-Bangladesh Moyitri Uddyan, Tripura, India Member, Editorial Board THE INCLUSIVE (On-Line Based International Journal To Be Launched In April 2012 From Kolkata) Former Editor And Publisher Samaj Chetona (Social Awareness) (A Bengali Journal On Politics And Development - A Total Of 63 Issues Published Between 1991-2001 From London And Dhaka) Former Newscaster And Producer Bangla Section, BBC World Service (1987-93) Life Members Bangla Academy, Dhaka Bangladesh Asiatic Society, Dhaka Paschimbanga Itihas Sangsad, Kolkata Association For Historical Studies, Kolkata North-East India History Association Television Talk Shows / Interviews In ATN Bangla, Desh TV, Ekushey, ATN News, Channel I, Bangla Vision, NTV, Somoi TV, Independent TV, Machhranga TV, Mohona TV. Interviews Or Interview-Based Reports Published In All Major National Dailies (Bangla And English) Special Interviews In: BBC World Service (Broadcasted From London, UK) Voice Of America (Broadcasted From Washington, USA) Himal (Published From Kathmandu, Nepal) Seven Sisters Post (Published From Gauhati In Assam, India)
